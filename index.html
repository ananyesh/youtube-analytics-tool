Frequently Asked Questions

Why is there no hourly data?

Public statistics are typically archived on a daily basis by major tracking services. Historical hourly data is not publicly archived for the majority of channels.

How far back can the Wayback Machine go?

It depends on when the channel was first archived. For popular channels, archives can go back as far as 2005.

Why do some logos fail to load?

Some older images may have been lost in the archive or are protected by CORS policies that even our proxies cannot bypass.
