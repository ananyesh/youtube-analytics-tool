Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the data?

We use high-precision tracking from multiple public APIs. For historical data, we use snapped snapshots, while our "Live" odometer uses advanced estimation algorithms to provide sub-second updates.

Why is there no hourly data for some dates?

Public statistics are typically archived on a daily basis. Historical hourly data is only available for periods where active tracking was enabled for that specific channel.

How does the Comparison tool work?

You can add up to 10 channels simultaneously. The graph automatically scales to handle channels of any size, allowing you to compare growth velocity between small and large creators.

How often does the dashboard update?

The "Live" statistics (Odometer and Header) update every 2 seconds. The growth charts update as new historical snapshots become available from our tracking partners.
